The history of craft beer

Craft beer arose in the United states sometime in the 1960's. THis came
about because homebrewing became popular around this same time so people
Started experimenting. Microbreweries became popular in the 90's and the rest
is history as you can find many breweries in each state and sometimes even each county.

History of patents

Over the years, brewers started to profit off of their beer but there were issued with copright
Brewers were finding people would copy a beer perfectly and sell it as thier own, so in the 90's
the national brewers associated started creating beer patent laws. This is a recurring process as their
are still laws being produced today with the most recent major law being introduced in 2019.
These laws outline basically what can be patented and what cant. It regulates so no single brewer can
patent a major ingredients of beer. It allows for the fairness of all brewers but allows brewers to profit
off of thier original creations.
